# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this project handles information.

## Information collection
- The software does not intentionally collect personal information by default.
- If you connect third-party services (e.g., networking, analytics), those services may collect data under their own policies.

## Local data
- Configuration and saved data may be stored locally on your device.
- You are responsible for securing your device and backups.

## Networked features
- Multiplayer or online features may transmit data such as usernames, session identifiers, and gameplay events to facilitate connectivity.
- Review and comply with the policies of any connected services.

## Cookies and tracking
- The software does not use cookies directly, but embedded services may use their own tracking technologies.

## Data sharing
- Data is not shared with the project authors except where you choose to submit logs or feedback.

## Security
- The project strives for reasonable security practices, but no method is 100% secure. Use at your own risk.

## Changes to this policy
- Updates may be made over time. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
